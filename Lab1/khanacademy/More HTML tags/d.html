Recipe #2

Beef stroganoff classic

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: _
Ingredients Quantity
Beef meat (loin, rump) 500 g.
Large onion 1 pc.
Flour 2 teaspoons
Sour cream 2-3 full Art. spoons
Vegetable oil for frying
Dill greens to taste

Step 1: Cut the meat into pieces two fingers thick. Repulse. Cut along the fibers into pieces no longer than 4-5 cm long.

Step 2: Peel the onion, finely chop, fry in a pan.

Step 3: Put the meat in the onion. Fry, stirring, over high heat for 5-7 minutes.

Step 4: Add flour, mix. Add 2/3 cup hot water or broth, simmer for 10 minutes.

Step 5:Add sour cream. Stir and simmer for 5 minutes.

Step 6: Serve beef stroganoff with mashed potatoes, sprinkle with dill. Rice, buckwheat can also serve as a side dish.

Recipe #3

Beef with bell pepper and soy sauce

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: _
Ingredients Quantity
Beef (pulp) 400 g.
Bulgarian red pepper 150 g (1 pc.)
Onion 90 g (1 pc.)
Egg 1 pc.
Soy sauce 3 tbsp. spoons
Salt 0.5 tsp
Ground black pepper a pinch
Starch 2 tbsp. spoons
Garlic 2-3 cloves
Ginger (root, crushed) 1 teaspoon
Vinegar 9% 1 teaspoon
Sugar 0.5 tsp
Vegetable oil (for frying) 60 ml (4 tablespoons)
Fresh parsley (for serving) 1-2 sprigs

Step 1: Prepare all ingredients. Any piece of beef without veins is suitable for the dish.

Step 2: The meat should be cut across the grain into strips as thin as possible. With a sharp knife, this is easy to do.

Step 3: Put the beef in a bowl, add the egg, 0.5 teaspoon of salt, ground black pepper and 1 tbsp. a spoonful of soy sauce.

Step 4: Also add 1 tbsp. a spoonful of starch, mix and leave the meat for 5-10 minutes.

Step 5: Peel the garlic and ginger and cut very finely with a knife (you can grate on a fine grater). Rinse parsley, shake and chop with a knife.

Step 6: Rinse sweet pepper, remove seeds. Cut the pepper into chunks. Cut the peeled onion into thin half rings or feathers.

Step 7: Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan and add half of the beef. The meat should be in a pan in one layer, so when quickly fried over high heat, it will retain all the juices.

Step 8: Fry the beef over high heat on all sides for 3-4 minutes, until the color changes and is lightly browned. Then transfer the fried meat to a plate and fry the next batch.

Step 9:Transfer the final batch of fried meat to a plate along with the oil and juice in which it was fried.

Step 10: In a skillet, heat 1-2 tbsp. tablespoons of vegetable oil and put the chopped pepper. Saute the peppers for 2 minutes, until soft.

Step 11: Add onions to the pan to the pepper, fry together for another 3-4 minutes.

Step 12: Transfer the meat to the pan along with the oil and juice. Add 1-2 tbsp. spoons of soy sauce, sugar and vinegar. Stir.

Step 13:1 st. dilute a spoonful of starch in 50 ml of drinking water and pour the mixture into the pan. Stir to coat all the ingredients in the sauce and simmer for 1 minute.

Step 14: Add chopped ginger and garlic to the pan, stir and turn off the heat.

Step 15: Sprinkle the contents of the pan with parsley. Beef with bell pepper and soy sauce is ready to serve.

Step 16: This meat is very tasty both cold and hot. This is a great oriental dish.
